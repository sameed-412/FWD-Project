Motivation to take the proposed project

Developing a live weather website can be a way to provide a valuable service to the community. It helps people stay informed about weather conditions, which is essential for safety and planning daily activities.
It can play a crucial role in emergency preparedness and response. During severe weather events, such a platform can provide real-time updates and alerts to help residents and authorities make timely decisions to protect lives and property.
Weather data collected and presented through a live website can also be valuable for scientific research. Meteorologists and climate scientists can use the data to study weather patterns and climate trends, contributing to our understanding of the Earth's climate system.
For regions dependent on tourism, a live weather website can attract visitors by providing accurate and up-to-date weather information. This can boost tourism and benefit the local economy.


Working

Revolves around the utilization of real-time weather data by using an Application Programming Interface(API) to provide accurate data which will then be displayed on the webpage.
By changing the time of day or the date till up to two weeks ahead, we can get the weather forecast. Utilization of an API helps provide the correct forecast with real-time updation of any sudden changes or warnings in the weather that may or may not occur


Advantages & disadvantages

Advantages:
Real-Time Information: Users can access up-to-the-minute weather information, which is crucial for planning daily activities, travel, and safety during severe weather events.
Convenience: It provides a convenient way for users to check weather conditions anytime, anywhere, using their computers or mobile devices.
Customisation: Users can often customise the website to receive weather information specific to their location, which enhances the user experience.

Disadvantages:
Accuracy: Weather predictions are not always 100% accurate, and users may become frustrated if they rely solely on website data for critical decisions.
Data Reliance: The project heavily depends on access to accurate and timely weather data, which may come with associated costs and data source limitations.
Competitive Market: The market for weather websites is competitive, with many established players, which can make it challenging to gain a significant user base.
